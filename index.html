Welcome to Wilson Lions PTA

The Wilson Elementary PTA is dedicated to enriching the educational experience of every student at Wilson Elementary in Payson, Utah. We bring together parents, teachers, and community members to support our school and our children.

Join us in making a difference! Whether you volunteer your time, attend events, or become a PTA member, your involvement matters.
